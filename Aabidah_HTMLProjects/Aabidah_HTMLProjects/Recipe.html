Jollof,Tilapia and salad Ghanaian dish

Ingredients

  • Long grain rice
  • Onions
  • Tilapia
  • Ripped Plantain
  • Lettuce
  • Carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Bell pepper
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Ginger powder
  • Curry Powder
  • Natural Spices(funnel, rosemary, bayleaf, corriander)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt

Preparation

    Jollof

  1. Heat a little amount of vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat, add 1 large Onions and stir until softened
  2. Add 8 chopped tomatoes, 3 blended fresh pepper, little amount of tomato paste, 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper, 1/2 teaspoon of curry powder, 1 teaspoon of ginger paste, 1 teaspoon of garlic paste, add 1 teaspoon of natural spices and add some salt to taste and 1 cube of the seasoning of your choice. Stir until properly mixed and cook until the tomatoes have softened and the oil has separated from the mixture.
  3. Add two cups of long grain rice and add a cup of water. Allow it to boil,then reduce to low heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes

    4. Vgetable salad

  4. wash and chop lettuce, tomatoes, onion, cucumber, and bell pepper. Combine and add them to large bowl.
  5. add 1/2 teaspoon of vegetable oil, a teaspoon of salt and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  6. Stir until properly coated and mixed

    7. Fried Plantain

  7. Heat a little amount of vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat
  8. Peel and slice the plantain to your desired size and wash with salty water
  9. Add the plantain to the oil and fry until it is golden brown

    10. Grilled tilapia

  10. pre heat the grill to medium heat
  11. Season the tilapia with salt, black pepper, garlic and onion paste
  12. Brush the tilapia with vegetable oil and cook through for about 4 to 5 minutes
  13. Serve the the jollof rice with vegetable salad, Fried plantain, and grilled tilapia and enjoy sumptuous your meal

This recipe is a healthy and balanced Ghanaian meal that is packed with nutrients and flavor. It is also a good source of protein, fiber, and vitamins. The jollof rice is a flavorful and filling dish that is made with rice, tomatoes, and peppers. The grilled tilapia is a lean source of protein that is cooked to perfection. The boiled greens are a good source of vitamins and minerals. The fried plantains are a delicious and healthy side dish that is made with ripe plantains.
